> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> Safety Precautions and PPE for operating
battery tools
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> Full PPE must be worn at all times.
•
Both inner and outer visors must be down
•
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> Gloves must be worn
•
Dust masks must be in place if glass is being cut or broken
•
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> If any tool is been used, hard protection must be in place for both the
casualty and the users.
•
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> Battery operated tools are a two person operation.(Lifting and/or hard
protection)
•
The tool casings are plastic therefore it is imperative that the rear of the
tool does not come under pressure from being wedged against any parts of
the vehicle.
•
Tools are not to be used as battering rams.
•
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> Do not stand between the tool and Vehicle when using the tools.
•
Inspect all tools/batteries and ancillary equipment for damage before and
after use.
•
All RTC procedures must be adhered to.

Battery Charger eWXT Indicator Lights
Top LED Light
The top LED light indicates the state of the charger.
•
A continuous Red light means the charger is on.
•
A flashing red light means the charger is faulty.
Bottom LED Light
The bottom LED light indicates the state of the
battery on charge.
•
A flashing green light means the battery is charging.
•
A continuous green light means the battery is fully charged.
•
A flashing red light means the battery is outside the
charging temperature range. When the correct temperature
range is reached the battery will commence charging.
•
A continuous red light means the battery is faulty.

9AH Battery
The 9.0 Ah battery has a quick release system with four
LED lights to indicate its charge status. It is placed on
the tool by sliding onto the rails. It is removed by
pressing the red button and sliding off the rails.
•
To check the battery status press the button above the
LED indicator lights.
•
Pressing the button again will turn the lights off.
•
When all four LEDs are on, the Battery is fully charged.
•
Quick Pressing the on button twice will turn on the
light at the rear of the battery pack enabling insertion
in darkened areas.
•
Place the battery on charge when the indicators are
red or amber.

LUKAS SILL SUPPORT
The LX CLAW set is designed to
support "CrossRamming". Cross-
Ramming is a technique used to
push deformed body panels back
to their original shape. By
increasing the surface area, it
ensures that any type of material
can be pushed away using the
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> ram. Claws on both sides ensure
perfect grip on all surfaces.
•
The red colour scheme is used for better visibility when working
in the vehicle.
•
The Ram can only be paced in the centre of the claw.
•
Can be used to increase the surface area at any point.
