The effects of undesired electromagnetic energy on electronic systems is called interference.

-
This occurs when a microphone and speaker are too close together and the sound will loop from the

Digital Noise
-
The operational of a radio nearby on a digital channel can cause interference for uses on the equivalent
analogue channel.

Signal Break Through

Caused when radio signals are been received from several direction causing distortion. Usually
occurring when the frequency of the channels are close together and the more powerful of the signals
from a different site breaks through.

Faraday Effect
•
A Faraday cage is essentially a container, or a shield, that blocks out electromagnetic radiation from
across the electromagnetic spectrum, such as radio waves and microwaves.
•
A reinforced concrete structure act as a very good Faraday cage
•
Almost everything in modern building construction either reflects, absorbs, refracts or diffracts radio
signals and creates a complex effect that attenuates incoming cellular and radio signals by as much as
60 to 99.99% . The result is that there is usually very little usable signal left inside most of the
building.
