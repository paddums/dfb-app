only by following the flushing procedure will the foam pump be

PROCEDURE FOR FLUSHING CAFS SYSTEM
1) Set throttle to idle
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> 2) Turn foam pump off ensure red border is not illuminated
3)
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**

4)
Turn flush lever to "on" position ( upper right side of pump bay) Foam Pump Flush
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> ( always ensure the Foam Pump is flushed after every use )
5)
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> Increase pump pressure to 3-4 bar (ensure that CAFS delivery valve is open)
flush system until water runs clear from the branch
> ⚠️ **SAFETY — Action > Condition > Standard**
> Ensure Both CAFS deliveries are flushed
BEWARE OF STORED PRESSURE IN THE UNUSED DELIVERY WHILST FLUSHING.
6) Close flush valve
7) Idle throttle and Disengage PTO
8) Close both delivery valves
9) Replenish foam tank

> 10)   Ensure all Blank Caps are Replaced on Deliveries and Foam pick up lines
