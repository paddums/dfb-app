Cylinder Duration
For a BA cylinder charged to a given pressure, calculate:
• cylinder capacity (liters)
• full duration (minutes)
• working duration (minutes)
• duration, wearer in entrapped procedure (minutes)

Full duration is from the moment the cylinder is opened until all the
contents are exhausted.

Turn Around Pressure

Turn around pressure is only ever to be used under the instruction of the
Officer in charge (OIC)
Calculation of Turn-Around Pressure
Half the cylinder contents at entry, then add 30 bar (this is half your safety
margin
Eg1.
300÷2= 150 bar
150+30= 180 bar (TAP)
Eg2.
270÷2=135 bar

135+30=165bar

Now Round up to 170 bar

(This ensures you err on the side of safety!)
